## Our journey in building for a billion Indians
### Aug 2015
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/mobile/journey/pizza-11.png)
We began with "Fashnear" - A hyperlocal fashion discovery platform
### Mar 2016
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/mobile/journey/pizza-10.png)
Pivoted to a mobile-first platform to help shop owners sell on WhatsApp & Facebook
### Jun 2016
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/mobile/journey/pizza-9.png)
Meesho is born, our founders get selected to Y Combinator
### Feb 2019
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/mobile/journey/pizza-11.png)
We hit 1.2 million monthly orders and ~200k transacting users
### Jun 2019
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/mobile/journey/pizza-7.png)
Facebook invests in us - first investment in an Indian startup
### Jul 2021
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/mobile/journey/pizza-4.png)
We become the first e-commerce platform to announce 0% commission for our sellers
### Sep 2021
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/mobile/journey/pizza-3.png)
We clock 51 million monthly orders & ~18 million transacting users. This is a 50x growth in 2 years
### Sep 2021
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/mobile/journey/pizza-2.png)
We’re the world’s most downloaded e-commerce mobile app
### April 2022
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-12.png)
Meesho Records Over 100 million Transacting Users
### June 2022
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-14.png)
Records 53.5 lakh orders during a one-day sale event
### Sep 2022
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-19.png)
Creates new record, clocks ~87.6 lakh orders on day one of Mega Blockbuster Sale
### Nov 2022
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-18.png)
Joins Open Network for Digital Commerce (ONDC) to help connect buyers with hyperlocal sellers
### Dec 2022
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-20.png)
Nearly 130,000 sellers became lakhpatis and 6,000 sellers became crorepatis on Meesho through the year
### Dec 2022
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-15.png)
Sees a record 91 crore orders placed in 2022
### Jan 2023
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-16.png)
We crossed 10 crore+ listings on the platform
### April 2023
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-17.png)
Meesho recorded 140 million annual transacting users on its platform – a sizable portion of India’s internet population
### Mar 2023
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-22.png)
Meesho became the fastest shopping app globally to cross 500 Mn downloads, as per data.ai
### June 2023
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/pizza-24.png)
Meesho earned the coveted spot on TIME’s 2023 list of 100 most influential companies
### Aug 2023
Becomes first horizontal e-commerce company to turn profitable
### September 2023
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/journey_updated_2025/Sept-2023-Mall.png)
### 400+ brands
Launched Meesho Mall, offering a curated selection for shoppers
### December 2023
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/journey_updated_2025/Dec-2023-non-GST-sellers.png)
### 25,000 non-GST sellers
Onboarded in just 2 months, reinforcing Meesho’s commitment to small business growth.
### August 2024
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/journey_updated_2025/Aug-2024-4%20ind-directors.png)
### Appointed 4 independent directors 
Strengthening Meesho’s leadership with experienced industry experts.
### October 2024
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/journey_updated_2025/Oct-2024-%E2%82%B9232%20Cr.png)
### ₹232 Cr
Became the first horizontal e-commerce company to achieve positive cash flow in FY24.
### November 2024
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/journey_updated_2025/Nov-2024-VoiceBot.png)
Meesho launches India’s First Gen AI-Powered Voice Bot at Scale
### December 2024
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/journey_updated_2025/Dec-2024-210Mn+%20downloads.png)
### 210 million+ downloads
Meesho retains its #1 spot as India’s most-downloaded shopping app for the fourth consecutive year.
### February 2025
![](https://www.meesho.io/img/home/journey8/journey_updated_2025/Feb-2024-21k+%20creators.png)
### 21,000+ creators
Meesho builds one of India’s largest creator marketplaces, driving content-led shopping at scale.
