# How to set up CNAME for Live stream or DVR
If you want to setup Cname for your playback URL instead of xxxxx-live.5centscdn.com you can enable it directly from our console. To do this you should set a CNAME or ANAME for your custom domain to xxxxx-live.5centscdn.com.
Click on "Live Streams"
